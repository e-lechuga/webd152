Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation int he practice of Yoga, This 60 minute class of poses and slow movement focuses on asana (proper alignment and posture) Pranayama ( breath work), and guided meditation to foster your mind and body connection
Vinyasa Yoga
Although designed for intermediate to advanced students, beginners are welcome to a sample this 60 minute class that focuses on breath-synchronized movement — you will inhale and exhale as you flow energetically though yoga poses
Restorative Yoga
This 90 minutes class features very slow moments and long posses that are supported by a chair or wall. This calming restorative experience is suitable for students of any level of experience. This practice can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate any injury
